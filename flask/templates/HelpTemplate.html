Measured Spectrum Occupancy Database (MSOD) Help

NOTE: Your MSOD session will time out if you do not click on the MSOD: Home tab after a short time.

Do not navigate using the browser forward and back arrows. Use the links on the menu bars on each page.
If the sensor map stops scrolling correctly please logout, close and re-open your browser and login for a new session.

Click on a topic to the left, or scroll down to browse the help sections.

SELECT A SENSOR

   You can select and view data from one sensor at a time.

   Sensor icons are placed on the map in locations where spectrum data is available. Properties of a sensor will pop up when you place the mouse cursor over a sensor icon.

  LTE sensor icons are teal.

  Radar sensor icons are orange.

   Select 'Show Markers by Freq Band' or 'Show Markers by Detected System' from the map page main menu bar to narrow down Sensor selection. Left click on a sensor icon to select that sensor.

   Information about the sensor you click on will appear to the left of the map:
     you must click on one of the available frequency bands listed there at the bottom to continue on to view or download data.

HOW TO DOWNLOAD DATA

   After selecting a sensor and frequency band to view, you can download data.

   Left click on the 'Download Data' item on the menu bar above the map. This will take you to the download data page.

   Wait until the data is ready: a link to download will appear on the page. If you prefer, click on the 'submit' button to receive the link to the data in an e-mail when the data is ready.

   To return to the map page, left click on the 'Available Sensors' item on the menu bar.

VIEW DATA: SELECTION OF START DATE AND DURATION

   After selecting a sensor and frequency band to view, you will see a map with sensor information listed to the left. If the frequency band you selected can be viewed in a sub-band, you can now enter the max and min frequency (MHz) for the sub-band of interest.

   Start date, number of days and number of aquisitions for the band you selected are shown above the map.

   From the menu bar above the map, left click on 'Generate Daily Occupancy Chart' to continue and view plots.

WHAT IS SHOWN ON THE PLOTS

   RADAR SENSOR

    After clicking on 'Generate Daily Occupancy Chart', you will see a plot of the daily occupancy.
    Left click on a point on the chart to see two tabs: one with a Single Day spectrogram and one with occupancy information.
    Left clicking on a point on the spectrogram or occupancy plot will open a tab with more information. Click on the tab to view that data.
    To navigate back through the plots, left click on one of the items in the menu bar: 'Available Sensors' >> 'Daily Occupancy'

   LTE SENSOR

    After clicking on 'Generate Daily Occupancy Chart', you will see a plot of the daily occupancy.
    Left click on a point on the chart, and you will see a plot of that day's occupancy.
    Left click on a point on the plot of Single Day Occupancy to see Acquisition Detail.
    The Acquisition Detail is given on two tabs: one with a spectrogram and the other with occupancy information.
    Left clicking on a point on the spectrogram or occupancy plot will open a tab with more information. Click on the tab to view that data.
    To navigate back through the plots, left click on one of the items in the menu bar: 'Available Sensors' >> 'Daily Occupancy' >> 'Single day Occupancy'
